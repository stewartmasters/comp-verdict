Evaluating a DevOps Engineer job offer in London? This page gives you salary benchmarks across four experience tiers so you can see exactly where your offer sits in the market. All figures are annual gross base salary in GBP, sourced from CompVerdict data (CV_DATA 2026-Q1).

## London DevOps Engineer Salary Benchmarks by Experience

The tables below show the 25th, 50th (median), and 75th percentile annual gross base salaries for DevOps Engineers in London. Use these to position your offer against the broader market.

Junior (0-2 years): P25 £35,500 | P50 £42,500 | P75 £52,000

Mid-level (3-5 years): P25 £58,000 | P50 £73,000 | P75 £91,500

Senior (6-10 years): P25 £85,500 | P50 £104,000 | P75 £131,000

Staff / Lead (10+ years): P25 £116,000 | P50 £146,000 | P75 £182,000

If your offer falls below the P25 for your tier, it is below what the majority of London employers are paying for comparable experience. An offer at or above the P75 is strong relative to the market.

## What These Percentiles Mean for Your Offer

P25 (25th percentile): One in four DevOps Engineers in London earns at or below this figure. An offer here is on the lower end of the market and may warrant negotiation.

P50 (median): Half of London DevOps Engineers earn above this figure, half below. A median offer is fair but leaves room to push higher if you have strong experience or in-demand skills.

P75 (75th percentile): Only one in four DevOps Engineers earns at or above this level. An offer here is genuinely competitive and reflects strong market positioning.

Always compare your offer to the tier that matches your actual years of experience, not just your job title, as responsibilities and scope vary significantly between employers.

## Junior DevOps Engineers in London (0-2 Years)

For those early in their DevOps career, the London market median sits at £42,500 gross per year. The lower quartile starts at £35,500, while the upper quartile reaches £52,000. If you are receiving your first or second DevOps offer, benchmark it against these figures before accepting. A significant gap below £35,500 is a signal to negotiate or reconsider. Data not available for equity, bonus, or benefits breakdowns at this tier.

## Mid-Level DevOps Engineers in London (3-5 Years)

Mid-level is the most active hiring band in the London DevOps market. The median salary is £73,000, with the interquartile range spanning £58,000 to £91,500. This wide spread reflects variation in employer type, tech stack specialisation, and sector. Cloud platform expertise, CI/CD tooling proficiency, and security knowledge are factors that typically push compensation toward the upper end of this range. Data not available for specific skill premium breakdowns.

## Senior and Staff-Level DevOps Engineers in London (6+ Years)

Senior DevOps Engineers (6-10 years) command a London median of £104,000, with the top quartile reaching £131,000. At the Staff or Lead level (10+ years), the median rises to £146,000 and the P75 reaches £182,000. At these levels, total compensation packages often include significant variable pay, but this page covers base salary only. Data not available for bonus, long-term incentive, or equity components. If your offer is structured with a lower base offset by variable pay, factor that into your comparison carefully.

## Key Factors That Affect Where Your Offer Falls

Several dimensions influence where a specific offer lands within these ranges. Employer type matters: large financial services firms and global tech companies tend to pay toward the upper quartile, while startups and public sector roles often sit closer to the median or below. Specialisation in areas such as Kubernetes, Terraform, AWS or Azure architecture, or platform engineering can shift compensation upward. Location within London (on-site requirements, hybrid flexibility) also affects how employers price roles. Data not available for sector-by-sector or skill-by-skill premium breakdowns in this dataset.
