Evaluating a Data Scientist job offer in Paris means knowing where your number actually lands in the market. This page breaks down annual gross base salary benchmarks by experience level so you can assess any offer with confidence. All figures are in euros (EUR), sourced from CompVerdict data (CV_DATA 2026-Q1), and reflect gross base pay only.

## How Paris Data Scientist Salaries Are Structured

Salaries for Data Scientists in Paris are quoted as annual gross base pay in euros. Bonuses, equity, and benefits are not included in these figures. The benchmarks below use three percentile markers: P25 (lower end of the market), P50 (the median, or midpoint), and P75 (the upper end). A competitive offer typically lands at or above P50 for your experience band. If your offer is below P25, it warrants serious scrutiny.

## Junior Data Scientist Salary in Paris (0-2 Years Experience)

For professionals with up to two years of experience, the Paris market shows the following annual gross base salary benchmarks: P25 is 29,580 EUR, P50 (median) is 37,740 EUR, and P75 is 47,430 EUR. If you are entering the field or recently graduated, an offer near the median of 37,740 EUR is a reasonable baseline. Offers below 29,580 EUR are below the lower quartile and should be questioned.

## Mid-Level Data Scientist Salary in Paris (3-5 Years Experience)

With three to five years of experience, compensation rises meaningfully. Paris benchmarks for this band are: P25 at 52,530 EUR, P50 at 66,300 EUR, and P75 at 85,170 EUR. The spread between P25 and P75 is over 32,000 EUR, reflecting significant variation based on industry, company size, and specialization. A strong offer in this band should approach or exceed 66,300 EUR.

## Senior Data Scientist Salary in Paris (6-10 Years Experience)

Senior Data Scientists in Paris command substantially higher compensation. Benchmarks for the six-to-ten year experience range are: P25 at 83,130 EUR, P50 at 104,040 EUR, and P75 at 126,480 EUR. At this level, an offer below 83,130 EUR is at the lower quartile and may undervalue your experience. The median of 104,040 EUR is a practical anchor for negotiation.

## Staff and Lead Data Scientist Salary in Paris (10+ Years Experience)

At the staff or lead level, with more than ten years of experience, Paris salary benchmarks are: P25 at 110,160 EUR, P50 at 136,680 EUR, and P75 at 164,220 EUR. Professionals at this level often carry team leadership, architecture ownership, or cross-functional responsibilities. An offer below 110,160 EUR for a genuine staff or lead role is worth challenging. Data not available for equity or long-term incentive benchmarks at this level.

## How to Use These Benchmarks to Evaluate Your Offer

Start by identifying your experience band, then locate your offer within the P25-P75 range. An offer at or above P50 is generally competitive for the Paris market.
