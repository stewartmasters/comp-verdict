If you're weighing a software engineer salary madrid job offer, knowing where your number sits in the market is the first step. This page breaks down annual gross base salary benchmarks for Software Engineers in Madrid across four experience tiers, so you can evaluate any offer with real data behind you.

## How to Read These Benchmarks

All figures below are annual gross base salary in euros (EUR), sourced from CV_DATA 2026-Q1. Three percentile markers are used throughout: p25 (lower end of market), p50 (median), and p75 (upper end of market). A competitive offer typically lands at or above p50 for your experience band. Figures represent base pay only — bonus, equity, and benefits are not included.

## Junior Software Engineer Salary in Madrid (0–2 Years Experience)

For engineers early in their careers, the Madrid market shows a fairly wide spread. At p25, annual gross base sits at €24,000. The median (p50) is €29,000, and top-quartile offers reach €36,500 at p75. If you have an offer below €24,000, it falls outside the typical market range for this tier. An offer at or above €29,000 is broadly competitive for a junior role in Madrid.

## Mid-Level Software Engineer Salary in Madrid (3–5 Years Experience)

Mid-level engineers see a significant step up in compensation. The p25 benchmark is €41,500, the median is €53,000, and the p75 sits at €65,500. The gap between p25 and p75 is over €24,000, reflecting how much specialisation, tech stack, and company type can influence pay at this stage. If your offer is below €41,500, it warrants a closer look before accepting.

## Senior Software Engineer Salary in Madrid (6–10 Years Experience)

Senior engineers in Madrid command meaningfully higher compensation. The p25 is €63,500, the median rises to €77,000, and p75 reaches €92,500. At this level, the difference between a median and upper-quartile offer is €15,500 per year — making negotiation leverage particularly valuable. Data not available for equity or long-term incentive breakdowns at this tier.

## Staff / Lead Software Engineer Salary in Madrid (10+ Years Experience)

At the staff and lead level, Madrid salaries reach their highest range in this dataset. The p25 benchmark is €85,500, the median is €104,000, and the p75 is €125,000. Offers at this tier can vary significantly based on scope of responsibility, team size, and whether the role carries people-management duties. Data not available for total compensation including equity at this level.
