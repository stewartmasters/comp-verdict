Evaluating a software engineer job offer in Singapore means knowing where your number sits in the real market. This page breaks down annual gross base salary benchmarks by experience level so you can assess any offer with confidence. All figures are in Singapore Dollars (SGD), annual gross base, sourced from CompVerdict data (CV_DATA 2026-Q1).

## Singapore Software Engineer Salary Benchmarks by Experience Level

The table below shows the 25th, 50th (median), and 75th percentile annual gross base salaries for software engineers in Singapore across four experience tiers. An offer at or above the 75th percentile is strong; below the 25th percentile warrants negotiation or closer scrutiny.

Junior (0-2 years): P25 SGD 50,000 | P50 SGD 64,500 | P75 SGD 83,000
Mid-Level (3-5 years): P25 SGD 89,500 | P50 SGD 114,000 | P75 SGD 146,000
Senior (6-10 years): P25 SGD 141,000 | P50 SGD 178,000 | P75 SGD 215,000
Staff / Lead (10+ years): P25 SGD 189,000 | P50 SGD 233,000 | P75 SGD 281,000

## Junior Software Engineers (0-2 Years Experience)

For engineers early in their careers, the median annual gross base salary in Singapore is SGD 64,500. A quarter of junior roles pay below SGD 50,000, while the top quartile reaches SGD 83,000 or more. If your offer falls below SGD 50,000, it is below the market floor for this tier and should be questioned. An offer near or above SGD 83,000 is a strong starting package for this experience band.

## Mid-Level Software Engineers (3-5 Years Experience)

Mid-level engineers see a significant jump in compensation. The median sits at SGD 114,000 annually, with the 25th percentile at SGD 89,500 and the 75th percentile at SGD 146,000. The gap between the 25th and 75th percentile is over SGD 56,000, reflecting meaningful variation based on specialisation, company type, and tech stack. If your offer is below SGD 89,500 at this experience level, it is below what a quarter of the market pays.

## Senior Software Engineers (6-10 Years Experience)

Senior engineers in Singapore command a median of SGD 178,000 per year. The lower quartile starts at SGD 141,000, and top-quartile earners reach SGD 215,000. Offers in this tier below SGD 141,000 should be carefully evaluated against total compensation, role scope, and growth trajectory. An offer above SGD 215,000 in base salary alone places you in the top quartile of the Singapore market for this level.

## Staff and Lead Engineers (10+ Years Experience)

At the staff and lead level, the median annual gross base salary reaches SGD 233,000. The 25th percentile is SGD 189,000 and the 75th percentile is SGD 281,000. This tier covers a wide range of roles including principal engineers, engineering managers, and technical leads. Data not available for further breakdown by sub-role (e.g., engineering manager vs. individual contributor staff engineer) within this tier.

## How to Use These Benchmarks to Evaluate Your Offer

Step 1: Identify your experience tier based on years of professional software engineering experience. Step 2: Locate your offer within the P25-P75 range for your tier. Step 3: An offer below P25 is below market for 75% of peers and is a clear signal to negotiate. An offer between P25 and P50 is below median but within range. P50 to P75 is competitive. Above P75 is a strong offer on base salary. Step 4: Base salary is one component. Data not available for equity, bonus, and benefits benchmarks in this dataset.
