If you are evaluating a data scientist salary zurich job offer, knowing where your number sits against the market is the first step to negotiating effectively. This page provides annual gross base salary benchmarks by experience level, sourced from CV_DATA 2026-Q1, so you can assess any offer with real figures rather than guesswork.

## How to Read These Benchmarks

All figures are annual gross base salary in Swiss Francs (CHF). They are presented at three percentile points: P25 (lower quartile), P50 (market median), and P75 (upper quartile). If your offer falls below P25 for your experience band, it is below what most employers in Zurich are paying. At P50 you are at the market midpoint. At or above P75 you are in the top quarter of earners for your level. Bonuses, equity, and benefits are not included in these figures. Data not available for total compensation breakdowns.

## Junior Data Scientist Salary Zurich (0–2 Years Experience)

Junior data scientists in Zurich can expect the following annual gross base salary range based on CV_DATA 2026-Q1:

P25: CHF 65,790 | P50: CHF 84,150 | P75: CHF 100,980

An offer below CHF 65,790 warrants scrutiny. The median of CHF 84,150 is a solid anchor for negotiation at this level. If you are coming in with a strong academic background or relevant internship experience, targeting closer to CHF 100,980 is reasonable.

## Mid-Level Data Scientist Salary Zurich (3–5 Years Experience)

With three to five years of experience, Zurich data scientists see a significant step up in compensation:

P25: CHF 116,280 | P50: CHF 139,740 | P75: CHF 166,260

The spread between P25 and P75 at this level is nearly CHF 50,000, reflecting how much specialisation, domain expertise, and company size can affect pay. If your offer is at or below CHF 116,280 and you have a proven track record, there is clear room to push back.

## Senior Data Scientist Salary Zurich (6–10 Years Experience)

Senior data scientists command some of the strongest compensation packages in Zurich's tech and finance sectors:

P25: CHF 161,160 | P50: CHF 202,980 | P75: CHF 246,840

The median crossing CHF 200,000 reflects Zurich's position as one of Europe's highest-paying markets for senior technical talent. ## Staff and Lead Data Scientist Salary Zurich (10+ Years Experience)

At the staff or lead level, total base compensation in Zurich reaches its highest tier:

P25: CHF 216,240 | P50: CHF 266,220 | P75: CHF 321,300

These figures apply to roles with significant scope — technical leadership, cross-functional influence, or management of data science teams. If a staff-level offer is below CHF 216,240, it is worth questioning whether the role scope and title are accurately aligned. ## Key Factors That Move Your Number

Benchmarks give you the market range, but several factors determine where within that range your offer should land. Industry matters: financial services and pharma firms in Zurich typically pay above the median, while early-stage startups may offer equity in lieu of base. Company size, the seniority of your direct stakeholders, and the specificity of your technical stack (e.g., deep learning versus general analytics) all shift the dial. Data not available for industry-specific or company-size breakdowns. Use the P25–P75 range as your negotiation corridor and anchor to P50 or above when you have competing offers or strong domain specialisation.
