If you are weighing a data scientist salary dublin job offer, knowing where the numbers land across experience levels is the first step to negotiating with confidence. This page breaks down annual gross base salary benchmarks for Dublin-based data scientists at the 25th, 50th, and 75th percentiles, sourced from CV_DATA 2026-Q1.

## How to Read These Benchmarks

All figures are annual gross base salary in euros, covering Dublin-based Data Scientist roles. The p25 marks the lower end of the market, p50 is the median, and p75 represents strong, competitive offers. If your offer sits below p25 for your experience band, it warrants scrutiny. At or above p75, you are in the top quarter of the market. These figures do not include bonuses, equity, or benefits, which can materially affect total compensation.

## Junior Data Scientist Salary Dublin (0–2 Years Experience)

For professionals early in their careers, Dublin's market shows a clear spread. At p25, junior data scientists earn €34,170 per year. The median (p50) sits at €44,880, and a strong offer at p75 reaches €56,610. If you are entering the field or transitioning from a related discipline, an offer below €34,170 is below market. Targeting the median or above is a reasonable baseline for negotiation.

## Mid-Level Data Scientist Salary Dublin (3–5 Years Experience)

Mid-level roles see a significant jump in compensation. The p25 benchmark is €62,220, the median is €79,050, and the p75 threshold is €102,000. This is the band where total compensation packages — including bonuses and stock — tend to become more meaningful. Evaluate your base offer against these figures before factoring in any variable pay. For comparison, [Software Engineer Salary Dublin: Evaluate Your Job Offer](/blog/software-engineer-salary-dublin-job-offer) covers the baseline role from which these figures are derived.

## Senior Data Scientist Salary Dublin (6–10 Years Experience)

Senior data scientists in Dublin command substantially higher compensation. The p25 sits at €98,430, the median at €124,440, and the p75 at €150,960. At this level, offers below €98,430 should be questioned unless offset by exceptional equity or other benefits. If you are evaluating offers across European markets, it is worth benchmarking against comparable cities: [Data Scientist Salary London: Evaluate Your Job Offer](/blog/data-scientist-salary-london-job-offer) and [Data Scientist Salary Berlin: Evaluate Your Job Offer](/blog/data-scientist-salary-berlin-job-offer) provide useful reference points.

## Staff and Lead Data Scientist Salary Dublin (10+ Years Experience)

At the staff and lead level, Dublin's market reflects the premium placed on deep expertise and leadership. The p25 benchmark is €132,600, the median is €163,200, and the p75 reaches €195,840. Professionals at this level are often evaluating offers that include significant equity components. Data not available for equity or bonus breakdowns at this level. Use the base salary figures here as your floor when negotiating.

## How to Use This Data to Evaluate Your Offer

Start by identifying your experience band, then locate your offer within the p25–p75 range. An offer at or above p50 is broadly competitive. Below p25 signals the offer is lagging the market and gives you clear grounds to negotiate upward. Above p75 indicates a strong offer, though you should still assess the full package including benefits, remote flexibility, and growth trajectory. These benchmarks reflect base salary only — data not available for total compensation norms by company type or sector within Dublin.
