## Negotiation Principles When Benchmarks Are Scarce

Absence of public benchmark data does not weaken your negotiating position — it shifts the burden of justification to the employer. Lead with the value you bring: your specific skill set, relevant experience, and market demand for your expertise. Ask the employer directly how the offered salary compares to their internal band for the role. If they decline to share a band, that itself is useful information.
