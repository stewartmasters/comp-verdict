If you're weighing a software engineer salary Stockholm job offer, knowing where your number sits in the market is the first step to negotiating with confidence. This page breaks down annual gross base salary benchmarks by experience level, sourced from CompVerdict's CV_DATA 2026-Q1 dataset, so you can assess any offer against real market percentiles.

## How to Read These Benchmarks

All figures are annual gross base salary in Swedish Kronor (SEK). They are organized into three percentile bands: P25 (lower market), P50 (market median), and P75 (upper market). A competitive offer typically lands at or above the P50 for your experience tier. An offer below P25 warrants scrutiny unless offset by strong equity, bonus, or other benefits. Data not available for total compensation including equity, bonus, or benefits.

## Junior Software Engineer (0–2 Years Experience)

For engineers early in their careers, Stockholm market benchmarks are as follows: P25 is SEK 308,000, P50 is SEK 397,000, and P75 is SEK 484,000 per year. If your offer falls below SEK 308,000, it is below the lower quartile of the market. An offer at or above SEK 484,000 places you in the top quarter of earners at this level.

## Mid-Level Software Engineer (3–5 Years Experience)

Engineers with three to five years of experience command a meaningfully higher range. The P25 sits at SEK 551,000, the median at SEK 673,000, and the P75 at SEK 836,000 annually. The jump from the junior median to the mid-level median represents a significant step up, reflecting the increased scope and ownership expected at this stage.

## Senior Software Engineer (6–10 Years Experience)

At the senior level, Stockholm benchmarks widen considerably, reflecting the variance in scope and company type. P25 is SEK 811,000, P50 is SEK 1,020,000, and P75 is SEK 1,238,000 per year. If you are evaluating a senior offer, a figure below SEK 811,000 suggests the employer is pricing below market. Crossing the SEK 1,000,000 threshold is standard at the median for this tier.

## Staff and Lead Software Engineer (10+ Years Experience)

For staff engineers, tech leads, and principal-level roles, the benchmarks are: P25 at SEK 1,087,000, P50 at SEK 1,338,000, and P75 at SEK 1,615,000 annually. At this level, total compensation structures can vary significantly by company. Data not available for equity or variable pay components at this tier. Base salary is only one dimension of a job offer. When evaluating a Stockholm offer, also consider pension contributions (Swedish employers are required to contribute to occupational pension), healthcare benefits, remote work flexibility, equity or profit-sharing structures, and annual bonus targets.
