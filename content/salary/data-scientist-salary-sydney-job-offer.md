If you're weighing a data scientist salary sydney job offer, knowing where your number sits in the market is the first step to negotiating with confidence. This page breaks down annual gross base salary benchmarks across four experience levels in Sydney, sourced from CompVerdict's CV_DATA 2026-Q1 dataset.

## How to Read These Benchmarks

All figures are annual gross base salary in Australian dollars. Each experience band shows three percentiles: p25 (lower quartile), p50 (median), and p75 (upper quartile). If your offer falls below p25 for your band, it warrants scrutiny. At or above p75 typically signals a strong offer. These figures cover base pay only and do not include bonuses, equity, or superannuation contributions.

## Junior Data Scientist (0–2 Years Experience)

Entry-level data scientists in Sydney see a wide spread depending on employer size and sector. The p25 sits at AUD 66,300, the median at AUD 85,170, and the p75 at AUD 109,140. Offers below AUD 66,300 are below the lower quartile of the market and should be questioned unless offset by significant equity or structured training programs.

## Mid-Level Data Scientist (3–5 Years Experience)

At the mid-level, compensation rises substantially. The p25 is AUD 117,300, the median is AUD 151,980, and the p75 reaches AUD 193,800. This band reflects professionals who can independently scope and deliver data science projects. If your offer is near or below AUD 117,300 with three or more years of experience, there is likely room to negotiate upward.

## Senior Data Scientist (6–10 Years Experience)

Senior practitioners command significantly higher pay. The p25 for this band is AUD 186,660, the median is AUD 235,620, and the p75 is AUD 285,600. Professionals at this level typically lead technical direction, mentor junior staff, and influence product or research strategy. An offer below AUD 186,660 for a genuinely senior role is below market by this dataset's measure.

## Staff or Lead Data Scientist (10+ Years Experience)

At the staff and lead level, Sydney salaries reach their highest range. The p25 is AUD 250,920, the median is AUD 309,060, and the p75 is AUD 373,320. Roles at this level typically carry cross-functional influence, technical leadership, or people management responsibilities. Use the p50 of AUD 309,060 as your anchor when evaluating principal or staff-level offers. ## How to Use This Data When Negotiating

Identify your experience band, locate your percentile, and anchor your counteroffer to the p50 or p75 as appropriate. If an employer's offer is below p25, ask for a written rationale or push for a structured review timeline. Total compensation matters too — superannuation, bonuses, and remote flexibility all affect the real value of an offer.
