## How to Read Your Percentile

The P50 (median) is the clearest reference point: half of Software Engineers at your experience level in Barcelona earn above this figure, and half earn below. If your offer lands between P25 and P50, it is below the median but not unusual — especially at smaller companies or startups. An offer above P75 signals that the employer is paying at the top of the Barcelona market for that tier. Use your specific experience level as the anchor, not a blended average across all levels.

The spread between experience tiers in Barcelona is significant. If you are a mid-level engineer being offered a junior-tier salary, the benchmarks give you a concrete basis for negotiation.
